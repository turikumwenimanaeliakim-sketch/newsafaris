Rwanda Itinerary Section

Gorilla Trekking Experience in Volcanoes National Park

Embark on a once-in-a-lifetime adventure to trek Rwanda's majestic mountain gorillas in Volcanoes National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage site.

Day 1: Arrival in Kigali & Transfer to Volcanoes National Park

Arrive in Kigali, Rwanda's capital, where you'll be welcomed by your tour guide. After a brief city tour and lunch, head towards Volcanoes National Park, located in the northwestern region of Rwanda. Check-in at your lodge, followed by an evening briefing on your upcoming trek. Relax and enjoy the stunning views of the volcanoes before your adventure begins.

Day 2: Gorilla Trekking Adventure

After an early breakfast, head to the park headquarters for a briefing on the gorilla trekking rules and safety. Accompanied by a knowledgeable guide, embark on your trek through the dense jungle to find one of the habituated gorilla families. Spend one unforgettable hour observing and photographing these incredible creatures in their natural habitat. After the trek, return to your lodge for a relaxing afternoon. Optional activities like a cultural visit to a local village are available.

Day 3: Golden Monkey Trekking & Dian Fossey Tomb Visit

On the third day, enjoy an exciting golden monkey trek, where you'll get the chance to see the rare and playful golden monkeys in the forest. In the afternoon, visit the tomb of Dian Fossey, the renowned primatologist who dedicated her life to the study and conservation of mountain gorillas. Learn about her remarkable legacy and the conservation efforts that have made gorilla trekking possible in Rwanda.

Day 4: Return to Kigali & Departure

After breakfast, take a scenic drive back to Kigali, where you'll have time for shopping at the local markets or a visit to the Genocide Memorial. Depending on your flight time, you'll be transferred to the airport for your departure, marking the end of an unforgettable gorilla trekking adventure.
