Akagera Wildlife Safari

Experience the wilderness of Akagera National Park with our expertly designed safari adventure. Witness the "Big Five" and immerse yourself in nature.

Day 1: Arrival in Akagera

Depart from Kigali and drive to Akagera National Park. Enjoy the scenic journey and settle into your lodge. In the afternoon, take part in a guided birdwatching tour along Lake Ihema.

Day 2: Game Drive and Boat Safari

Embark on an early morning game drive to spot lions, elephants, giraffes, and other wildlife. In the afternoon, enjoy a boat safari on Lake Ihema, home to hippos, crocodiles, and a variety of birds.

Day 3: Full Day Game Safari

Spend a full day exploring Akagera's diverse landscapes. Visit the northern section for a higher chance of spotting elusive species like leopards and rhinos. Return to the lodge in the evening for a bonfire dinner.
