Rwanda Itinerary Section

7 Days Best of Rwanda Safari

Experience the highlights of Rwanda, from breathtaking wildlife safaris to cultural tours and scenic landscapes. This 7-day adventure combines the best that Rwanda has to offer.

Day 1: Arrival in Kigali

Arrive at Kigali International Airport and meet your guide. Enjoy a brief city tour, including a visit to the Kigali Genocide Memorial. Overnight stay at a boutique hotel in Kigali.

Day 2: Transfer to Akagera National Park & Afternoon Game Drive

Drive to Akagera National Park in the morning. After check-in at the lodge, embark on an afternoon game drive to spot elephants, lions, giraffes, and zebras. Relax and enjoy dinner at the lodge.

Day 3: Full-Day Safari in Akagera National Park

Spend the day exploring the park on a game drive. Witness the "Big Five" and other wildlife in their natural habitats. Take a boat ride on Lake Ihema in the afternoon. Overnight at the lodge.

Day 4: Transfer to Volcanoes National Park

After breakfast, journey to Volcanoes National Park. Stop at scenic spots along the way. Upon arrival, visit local cultural sites, such as the Iby‚ÄôIwacu Cultural Village. Overnight at a luxury lodge near the park.

Day 5: Gorilla Trekking Adventure

Embark on a once-in-a-lifetime gorilla trekking experience. Spend an hour with the majestic mountain gorillas in their natural habitat. Return to the lodge for relaxation and a celebratory dinner.

Day 6: Visit to Lake Kivu

Travel to Lake Kivu and enjoy a relaxing day by the water. Participate in optional activities like kayaking or a boat ride to nearby islands. Explore Rubavu town and its vibrant markets. Overnight stay at a lakeside lodge.

Day 7: Return to Kigali & Departure

Drive back to Kigali with optional stops for shopping or a coffee plantation tour. Transfer to the airport for your departure flight, ending your incredible Rwanda adventure.
