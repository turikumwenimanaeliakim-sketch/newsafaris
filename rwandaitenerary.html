Rwanda Tour Itinerary

Discover the beauty of Rwanda with our curated travel packages designed to give you unforgettable experiences.

Day 1: Arrival in Kigali

Welcome to Rwanda! Our team will meet you at Kigali International Airport and transfer you to your hotel. Enjoy a guided city tour including the Kigali Genocide Memorial.

Day 2: Journey to Volcanoes National Park

Experience the scenic countryside as you travel to Volcanoes National Park. Visit local communities and cultural sites upon arrival. Stay at Volcano Lodge for the night.

Day 3: Gorilla Trekking Adventure

Embark on a breathtaking gorilla trekking experience in Volcanoes National Park. Witness mountain gorillas in their natural habitat for an unforgettable experience.
