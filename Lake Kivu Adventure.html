Lake Kivu Adventure

Immerse yourself in the beauty and tranquility of Lake Kivu, with visits to picturesque towns and villages along its shores.

Day 1: Arrival in Kibuye (Karongi)

Start your journey with a scenic drive from Kigali to Kibuye. Explore the town's stunning lakeside views, visit Ndaba Waterfalls, and enjoy an evening boat ride on Lake Kivu. Overnight stay at a lakeside lodge.

Day 2: Discover Cyangugu (Rusizi)

Travel south to Cyangugu, a serene town near the border of the Democratic Republic of Congo. Visit Kamembe Market for a cultural experience, and enjoy the panoramic views of Lake Kivu's southern end. Stay at a boutique hotel in Rusizi.

Day 3: Rubavu Village Walk and Relaxation

Drive north to Rubavu, where you'll take part in a guided village walk to learn about local traditions and lifestyles. Spend the afternoon relaxing on the beach or taking part in optional water sports. Return to your lodge for a farewell dinner.
