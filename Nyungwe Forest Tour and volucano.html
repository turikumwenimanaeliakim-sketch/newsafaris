Nyungwe Forest & Volcanoes National Park Tour - February 2025

Experience the lush beauty of Nyungwe Forest and the thrill of gorilla trekking in Volcanoes National Park. A combined tour to explore Rwanda's most stunning natural wonders.

Day 1: Arrival in Kigali & Transfer to Nyungwe Forest

Arrive in Kigali and meet your guide. After a brief city tour and lunch, depart for Nyungwe Forest, one of the largest montane rainforests in Central Africa. This UNESCO World Heritage site is home to diverse wildlife and rich plant species. Check-in at your lodge and enjoy the tranquil forest surroundings. In the evening, enjoy a delicious dinner at your lodge.

Day 2: Canopy Walk & Nature Trek in Nyungwe Forest

Start your day early with a guided canopy walk over the Nyungwe Forest‚Äôs treetops for a breathtaking view of the rainforest below. Afterward, enjoy a nature trek to spot wildlife such as colobus monkeys, chimpanzees, and various bird species. The trail will lead you through the dense forest, where you can learn about its unique biodiversity. In the afternoon, visit the nearby tea plantations to see Rwanda‚Äôs famous tea production in action.

Day 3: Transfer to Volcanoes National Park & Afternoon Exploration

After breakfast, drive north towards Volcanoes National Park. Along the way, enjoy the scenic views of Rwanda‚Äôs rolling hills and rural landscapes. Upon arrival, check into your lodge in the Volcanoes region. In the afternoon, you have the option to explore the nearby areas, such as the Iby'iwacu Cultural Village, or take a short hike around the park's base. Relax and enjoy a hearty dinner at your lodge in preparation for your gorilla trek the next day.

Day 4: Gorilla Trekking & Cultural Visit

After an early breakfast, head to the Volcanoes National Park headquarters for a briefing on the gorilla trekking rules and safety. Embark on your trek into the forest, where you will spend an unforgettable hour with one of the habituated gorilla families. Witness these majestic creatures in their natural habitat, a truly life-changing experience. In the afternoon, visit the nearby Dian Fossey Tomb for a fascinating insight into the life of the famous primatologist and the conservation of mountain gorillas. Return to your lodge for a well-deserved rest.

Day 5: Golden Monkey Trekking & Transfer to Kigali

On your last day, take part in a golden monkey trekking adventure in Volcanoes National Park, another rare and exciting experience in the area. Afterward, begin your drive back to Kigali. Depending on your arrival time, you can explore the local markets or shop for souvenirs. Enjoy a farewell lunch in Kigali before being transferred to the airport for your departure.
