Rwanda Itinerary Section

Karisimbi & Bisoke Hiking Adventure

Explore the stunning landscapes of Rwanda with this itinerary featuring mountain hikes, cultural experiences, and cave explorations.

Day 1: Arrival in Musanze

Arrive in Musanze town, your gateway to the Volcanoes National Park. Check-in at your hotel and enjoy an orientation about the exciting activities ahead. Overnight stay at a comfortable lodge.

Day 2: Mount Karisimbi Hiking ‚Äì Day 1

Begin your two-day hike of Mount Karisimbi, the highest peak in Rwanda. Trek through dense forests and volcanic terrain while enjoying breathtaking views of the Virunga ranges. Camp overnight on the mountain under the stars.

Day 3: Mount Karisimbi Hiking ‚Äì Day 2

Continue your trek to the summit of Mount Karisimbi, reaching the peak for stunning panoramic views. Descend back to the base and transfer to your lodge for relaxation and recovery.

Day 4: Mount Bisoke Hiking

Embark on a day hike to Mount Bisoke. Witness the iconic crater lake at the summit and marvel at the lush vegetation and diverse wildlife along the trail. Return to your lodge for an overnight stay.

Day 5: Dian Fossey Tomb Visit

Take a guided hike to the Dian Fossey Tomb and Research Center. Learn about her incredible work in gorilla conservation and her enduring legacy in protecting mountain gorillas. Spend the afternoon relaxing at your lodge.

Day 6: Musanze Caves Exploration & Kinigi Village Walk

Start your day exploring the Musanze Caves, an extraordinary geological formation with a rich history. In the afternoon, enjoy a guided walk through Kinigi Village to learn about local culture, traditions, and crafts. Overnight stay at your lodge.

Day 7: Departure

Enjoy your breakfast and prepare for your departure. Optional activities or shopping can be arranged depending on your flight schedule. Transfer to Kigali or another destination of your choice.
